"Mastaney - this Punjabi movie stands as a groundbreaking achievement in the realm of Punjabi entertainment. This much-loved Punjabi movie has transcended the usual comedic fare to historical action drama. People have eagerly anticipated the release of this must-watch Punjabi movie on OTT. Abiding by the moto to get the latest movie for our viewers, Mastaney is all set to release on Chaupal on November 9th.

Mastaney is set against the backdrop of 1739. It delves into the unexpected clash between Nader Shah's formidable army and a faction of Sikh rebels. This Punjabi film weaves a fictitious narrative around five ordinary men who are thrust into the roles of Sikh rebels, ultimately undergoing a transformative journey that unravels the true essence of Sikhism.

Through meticulous storytelling, "Mastaney" draws inspiration from the teachings of revered Sikh warrior gurus like Guru Hargobind Singh, Guru Teg Bahadur, and Guru Gobind Singh, etching a vivid portrait of Sikh bravery. The narrative in this Punjabi movie kicks off with the comic element of four commoners, including Zahoor (Tarsem Jassar) and Bashir (Karamjit Anmol), showcasing their buffooneries as they're enlisted by Zakariya Khan's wazir (Avtar Gill) to masquerade as Sikhs. As the plot gets towards its climax, the entry of a fakir named Kalander (Gurpreet Ghuggi) ignites a spark of Sikh courage within them. Kalander tells them tales of Sikh heroism and the transformative power of ardaas, leading to a stark transition from commoners to warriors.

Starring the talented Tarsem Jassar, Gurpreet Ghuggi, Rahul Dev, along with Karamjit Anmol, Simi Chahal, Baninder Bunny, Honey Mattu, Avtar Gill, and Arif Zakaria, "Mastaney" boasts powerful performances that breathe life into all the characters, providing an authentic touch to the historical narrative.

Gurpreet Ghuggi, a stalwart of Punjabi cinema since 2002, has been part of many iconic films.
