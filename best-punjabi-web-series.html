Dive Deep into Punjabi Entertainment: Best Punjabi Web Series to Watch

Sat Shri Akal Ji! Feeling the mid-day blues or seeking late-night entertainment? Whether you're flying solo or cuddled up with a loved one, Chaupal has you covered with a plethora of gripping web series. Dive into a world brimming with thrill, drama, and heartfelt emotions—a complete package to keep you captivated from start to finish, ensuring you're perched on the edge of your seat throughout the journey. Here's a curated list of Punjabi web series that promises to leave you hooked, begging for more.

  1. Plaster – Five young men who dream of achieving something big find themselves entangled in the world of drugs and the underworld, but a mishap with a drug deal turns their lives upside down.
  2. Shikaari - Three friends Jeeta, Mahinder, and Major, now in their old age wish to complete their bucket list from their teen years. But things get a little out of the way for this rowdy trio, when a police officer, Randhawa, tries to curb them.
  3. Zila Sangrur - This trio of Babbal Rai, Prince Kanwaljit Singh, and Raghveer Boli will rule over the city of Chandigarh but things will take a turn for worse for them as they kill a companion of Balbir, a prominent figure of Chandigarh. It will be an interesting thing to know whether they will take themselves out of this mess or will lose something or someone important.
  4. Vaardaat - The discovery of MLA Singh's son, Samar, murdered throws the city into a frenzy. The prime suspects? Three lowlifes known for petty theft were caught fleeing the scene with two insignificant bags. But there is a glimmer of hope for them - a missing pen drive rumored to hold Samar's secrets. Is the pen drive the key that unlocks a web of lies, or a desperate attempt to divert attention from a more sinister motive?
  5. Shahi Majra - A small-town girl, Agam, leaves her simple life for the excitement of Chandigarh to get an education. The city's energy and possibilities are alluring, but they also hide dangers. Can Agam overcome the challenges of city life and emerge stronger?
  6. Outlaw - Starring Gippy Grewal, Tanu Grewal, Yograj Singh, and Prince Kanwaljit Singh, 'Outlaw' is a crime thriller series about the duo of Chacha and Bhatija, who are determined to rule over the whole of Canada. Will they succeed as new secrets come to light?
  7. Murabba - Obsessed with land, a village descends into chaos as wealthy landowners become consumed by acquiring a coveted 25-acre plot (Murabba) for themselves. Their relentless pursuit of this dream leads them down a dark path, where morals are cast aside in favor of achieving their ultimate goal.
  8. 500 Meter - A child is abducted and tragically killed, with the perpetrator now on the loose, leaving behind only a single lead. Officer Rajveer faces a race against time to apprehend the killer before it's too late. Will he be able to win against all the odds?
  9. Ki banu punia da - Harman and Rajveer defy the wishes of Rajveer's father, Balwant Singh Punia, by marrying each other. To compound matters, they opt to temporarily care for a child, leading to a series of chaotic events.
  10. Range - Jaskaran, once a celebrated athlete with a gold medal, became consumed by revenge for his father's murder. However, a life of crime proved to be a dark path, leaving him trapped and overwhelmed.
  11. Gangland in Motherland - The growing power of student gangs and their fierce rivalries highlight a disturbing trend of youth violence in Punjab.
  12. Chandigarh wale - Charan and Dimple's new life in Chandigarh turns into a nightmare thanks to a never-ending flow of bothersome visitors
  13. Jubliee - Three tough guys are in a sticky situation. They're after a super valuable thing called "Jubliee," but things get even trickier when they bring their own children along for the ride.

So, what are you waiting for? Dim the lights, grab your favorite snacks, and dive into Chaupal's incredible selection of shows. Get ready to lose yourself in these captivating Punjabi web series - it's time for a Chaupal marathon!
