Brown Boys Go Wild : Gear up for Gippy Grewal's OUTLAW!!

Get ready for the ultimate GANGSTA feel as "OUTLAW", featuring our very own desi rockstar Gippy Grewal is all set to take you on a thrilling journey through the dark world of crime and betrayal.

Plot unveiled: "OUTLAW" takes you on a thrilling ride through the captivating landscapes of Canada and the downtown - a side less covered by most. Gippy Grewal's first-ever Punjabi web series is about a Chacha-Bhatija duo determined to rule Canada's scary world of crime. The Punjabi series "OUTLAW" revolves around the greed to earn whatever possible instant money. Each episode is actually a package of high-octane drama, suspense, and class action, giving you an exciting and immersive experience that will leave you wanting more when you have binged the entire web series.

Power-Packed Performances: "OUTLAW" features an ensemble cast of talented performers Gippy Grewal as Kala, Yograj Singh as Mewa Singh & the very versatile Prince Kanwaljit Singh, AKA Jeeta.

The ultimate Chemistry of "Yaar Mera Titliaan Warga" pair Tanu Grewal and Gippy Grewal bring smiles to viewers' faces through the thick and thin of high-octane action drama. Adding to the ensemble, Raj Jhinjar, known for his subtle acting in many Punjabi movies, delivers yet another remarkable performance as Kala's cousin and Mewa's own dutiful son.

However, the spotlight is again mounted sharply on Prince Kanwaljit, who takes on yet another challenging role of an addicted taxi driver named Jeeta. Kanwaljit's dedication to his craft is evident as he skillfully brings Jeeta's to life and helps draw some relatability to many audiences doing menial jobs to run their kitchens in Canada. Prince Kanwaljit aces the parts where a simple conversation becomes an echoing dialogue because he has said it in his own style.

Along with the lead actors, Gippy Grewal and Yograj Singh, Prince Kanwaljit's captivating portrayal as Jeeta and the compelling performances of Raj Jhinjar and Tanu Grewal, "OUTLAW" promises to be a giant leap in the universe of Punjabi web series.
